* Why emacs org-mode?
** Consistent markup language with export support to multiple file types:
  * Latex
  * Beamer
  * HTML
  * Word
  * PDF
  * and many more
** Time efficiency
  * Mouse-free workflow
  * Highly customizable through e-lisp
* How do I blog with emacs and org-mode?
** Getting Started
